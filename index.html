Clean Clothes for Asheville


Inspired by the teachings of Pope Francis, Mother Theresa, Jesus Christ and the founder's grandmother, Marie Sevier, Clean Clothes for Asheville strives to help the marginalized of society by providing means to launder their clothes. On Wednesday nights 5 pm - 8 pm at Dutch Girl on Haywood Rd in West Asheville, Clean Clothes provides funds and soap for the homeless to do laundry. Join us in our mission by volunteering or fundraising to help others help themselves.
